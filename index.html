GeoQuizMaster

The Ultimate Geography Trivia Challenge

GeoQuizMaster is an engaging educational game designed to test your knowledge of the world. With multiple modes including sudden death, identify countries by their flags, shapes, capitals, and even dive deep into state-specific trivia. Explore rich interactive maps while tracking your progress.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

At Elderlabs, we value your privacy and aim to be completely transparent about how your data is handled. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding your information when you use the GeoQuizMaster application.

Information We Collect

GeoQuizMaster is built to prioritize your privacy. We do not require you to create a user account to play, and we do not actively collect personal identifying information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

When you use the app, some non-personal information may be generated:

  • Gameplay Data: Your scores, high scores, unlocked achievements, and general game progress. This data is stored strictly locally on your own device.
  • Device Diagnostics: Basic, anonymized system information (such as operating system version) may be used to help us crash-test and improve the app stability.

Third-Party Services & Advertising

To keep the development of our games sustainable, GeoQuizMaster displays advertisements through integrated third-party networks, specifically Google AdMob.

  • Google AdMob: The AdMob SDK may collect anonymous device parameters, advertising IDs, and interaction data to serve personalized and relevant ads.

You have control over personalized advertising. You can opt out at any time by navigating to your device's system settings (e.g., selecting "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

Data Retention & Children's Privacy

Because your game progress is saved directly on your device, deleting the GeoQuizMaster app will automatically erase all your localized save data. Our application is safe for players of all ages, but we do not knowingly solicit or collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy or your overall experience with GeoQuizMaster, please feel free to reach out to us at privacy@elderlabs.example.
